Physiotherapy Services

Physiotherapy is a specialized healthcare service designed to help individuals regain strength, improve mobility, and reduce pain caused by injury, surgery, chronic illness, or age-related conditions. At Sibi Homecare Services, we offer personalized, home-based physiotherapy sessions to support recovery and enhance overall well-being in the comfort of your home.

Services offered in Physiotherapy

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Guided exercises to regain strength and function

Stroke & Neurological Rehabilitation

Post-surgical and chronic wound management.

Pain Management Therapy

Reducing chronic pain from arthritis, back pain, and joint issues.

Injury Recovery & Sports Therapy

Aiding recovery from fractures, sprains, and muscle injuries.

Elderly & Fall Prevention

Strength and balance exercises to enhance stability.

Customized Exercise & Mobility Programs

Designed to improve flexibility, coordination, and strength.
