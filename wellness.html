Wellness Checks & Chronic Disease Management (CDM) Packages

At Sibi Homecare Services, we believe in preventive healthcare and proactive disease management Our Wellness Check packages help individuals monitor their overall health, while our Chronic Disease Management (CDM) programs provide ongoing monitoring and support for individuals with long-term conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, and kidney conditions.

Our Wellness Check Packages

Basic Wellness Check (Essential Health Screening)

✔ Blood Pressure & Heart Rate Check

✔ Blood Sugar Test (Diabetes Screening)

✔ Body Mass Index (BMI) & Weight Analysis

✔ General Health Consultation

Standard Wellness Check (Routine Health Monitoring)

✔ All Basic Package Tests.

✔ Complete Blood Count (CBC) – Checks for infections, anemia, and overall blood health.

✔ Cholesterol & Lipid Profile – Heart health and risk assessment.

✔ Kidney & Liver Function Tests.

✔ Urinalysis – Checks for infections and kidney health.

Comprehensive Wellness Check (Full-Body Health Assessment)

✔ All Standard Package Tests.

✔ Thyroid Function Tests (T3, T4, TSH).

✔ Vitamin & Mineral Deficiency Screening.

✔ Cancer Marker Screening (Based on risk factors & age).

✔ Cardiac Health Tests (ECG/Advanced Lipid Profile).

✔ Hormone Profile for Men & Women.

VVIP Wellness Check (Executive & Personalized Health Screening)

✔ All Comprehensive Package Tests.

✔ Advanced Cancer Screening (PSA, CA-125, etc.).

✔ Full-Body Imaging & Diagnostic Scans (Ultrasound, X-ray, MRI/CT where necessary).

✔ Genetic Risk Assessment for Chronic Diseases.

✔ Specialist Consultation & Personalized Health Plan.

✔ Nutrition & Lifestyle Coaching.

Chronic Disease Management Packages

For individuals with diabetes, hypertension, kidney disease, heart conditions, and other chronic illnesses, we offer regular monitoring and personalized care plans to help improve quality of life and prevent complications. CDM packages can be scheduled monthly, quarterly, twice a year, or as prescribed by a doctor.

CDM Packages include
✔ Regular Blood Sugar & Blood Pressure Monitoring
✔ Lipid Profile & Heart Health Tests
✔ Kidney & Liver Function Monitoring
✔ HbA1c Test (For Diabetes Control)
✔ Weight, BMI & Nutrition Assessment
✔ Medication Adherence Support
✔ Doctor & Specialist Consultations (where required)
✔ Home-Based Care & Lifestyle Coaching
